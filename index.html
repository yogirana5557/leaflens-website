How it works

Three steps to a healthier plant.

No expertise needed. Just point, snap, and follow the plan.

1

Snap a Photo

Take a photo of any leaf, stem, or the whole plant using your camera or pick from your gallery.

2

Get Diagnosed

Claude AI analyses the image in seconds and identifies exactly what's wrong — disease, pest, deficiency, or all three.

3

Follow the Plan

Get a clear, actionable treatment plan with causes, remedies, and prevention tips so it never happens again.

4

Track Recovery

Re-scan as your plant heals. Watch the health score climb and build a full diagnosis history over time.
